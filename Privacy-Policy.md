<h1>Privacy Policy</h1>

<p>
Real Internet Speed Meter ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information.
This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects information when you use our services.
</p>

<h2>Information Collection and Use</h2>

<p>
The App itself does <strong>not collect, store, or share</strong> your personal information.
</p>

<p>
To provide its core functionality, the App accesses network traffic statistics available through the Android operating system to display:
</p>

<ul>
<li>Real-time download speed</li>
<li>Real-time upload speed</li>
<li>Internet speed in the status bar and notification</li>
</ul>

<p>
The App does not collect your browsing history, passwords, contacts, messages, photos, videos, or precise location.
</p>

<h2>Permissions Used</h2>

<ul>
<li><strong>FOREGROUND_SERVICE</strong> – Required to continuously display real-time internet speed in a notification.</li>

<li><strong>ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE</strong> – Used to detect network connectivity.</li>

<li><strong>POST_NOTIFICATIONS</strong> (Android 13+) – Used to display the speed notification.</li>

<li><strong>AD_ID</strong> – Used by Google AdMob to provide relevant advertisements.</li>
</ul>
